Join BeagleBoard.org at Embedded World Exhibition and Conference, February 26-28, 2019 in Nürnberg, Germany.

Stop by Booth 3A-121 to meet the new additions to BeagleBoard.org® family of products.

Also, check out the  latest demos of BeagleBoard.org® platforms including PocketBeagle® and BeagleBone® Blue. Now in our 10th Year, BeagleBoard.org® facilitates new embedded applications from rapid prototyping for AI, to an Industrial project or a small IoT device.

Register Now for the Hands-On Coding with Embedded Linux Workshop with BeagleBoard.org® PocketBeagle®, February 27, 2019 from 2:30-5:30pm. This hands-on, getting started workshop with PocketBeagle® will cover the basics of coding with open-source Linux based software using online IDE Cloud9 and adding sensors and outputs; from add on boards to circuits- try your hand at your own design.

Meet with BeagleBoard.org® at Embedded World: Schedule a meeting at the show with Jason Kridner, our co-founder or Christine Long, our Executive director. Use the link below to pick a day and time. Meetings will be held at Hall 3A / Stand 3A-121.
