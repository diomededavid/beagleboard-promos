PocketBeagle® is here and ready for the Holidays
 
 
 
 

It's here and ready for the Holidays. BeagleBoard.org® PocketBeagle® is a small mint tin board and the perfect size for a stocking. PocketBeagle® features an incredible low cost, slick design, and simple usage, making PocketBeagle® the ideal development board for beginners and professionals alike.
 
Who is using PocketBeagle®?
  • Hobbyists
  • Educators
  • Professionals
Why are they using it?
  • Low cost Linux computer
  • Great opportunity to learn programming with Python
  • Openness and flexibility tear down limits on your imagination

About BeagleBoard.org®

BeagleBoard.org® is a 501c3 and was founded in 2008 by a group of enthusiastic engineers interested in creating powerful, open and embedded devices. BeagleBoard.org® pioneered new ground in open hardware/software and community education. Visit https://www.beagleboard.org/ or email christi@beagleboard.org to get started.
