PocketBeagle® is here and ready for the Holidays
 
 
 

Dear Santa, would you please bring me a PocketBeagle?

BeagleBoard.org® PocketBeagle® is a small development board the size of a mint tin, perfect for a stocking stuffer. PocketBeagle® features an incredible low cost, slick design, and easy to follow instructions with projects for beginners and professionals alike.

 
Who is using PocketBeagle® to build with electronics and learn programming to light up their Holiday Projects and more?
  • Hobbyists
  • Educators
  • Professionals
 
What are projects are they building this Holiday Season?
  • PocketBeagle® Wearable Tops Off Your Holidays
  • Rose-Hulman ECE Christmas Lights
  • Keep Santa's Coffee Warm
 

Build Your Own Games, Robotics and Holiday Projects:

  • Smart Home Music Player
  • Wearables and Art
  • Quad copters and Race cars

About BeagleBoard.org®

BeagleBoard.org® is a 501c3 and was founded in 2008 by a group of enthusiastic engineers interested in creating powerful, open and embedded devices. BeagleBoard.org® pioneered new ground in open hardware/software and community education. Visit https://www.beagleboard.org/ or email christi@beagleboard.org to get started.
